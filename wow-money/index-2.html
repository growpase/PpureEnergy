Wow Money, What is your money story?

Our reality is shaped by the stories we tell ourselves. If you believe that people are dishonest, you might attract dishonesty into your life.
Your story might be that you struggle to trust others and find it difficult to decide whom to trust. This lack of trust could lead to strained relationships and missed opportunities.
To transform your relationship with money, you need to understand your money story. Here are 21 common money stories that might be holding you back:
  1. The story of scarcity
  2. The story of money-related trauma
  3. The story of poverty mindset
  4. The story of deprivation and missed opportunities
  5. The story of feeling stuck
  6. The story of self-doubt in creating wealth
  7. The story of inner pain related to money
  8. The story of accumulating debt instead of wealth
  9. The story of jealousy towards others' wealth
  10. The story of feeling like a victim of circumstances
  11. The story of repressing your desires
  12. The story of being exploited in financial matters
  13. The story of always feeling lacking
  14. The story of facing inequality in financial matters
  15. The story of misery associated with money
  16. The story of holding magical beliefs about creating wealth
  17. The story of guilt around money matters
  18. The story of a survival mindset when it comes to making money
  19. The story of blaming others or oneself for financial struggles
  20. The story of ancestral burdens and legacy issues with money
  21. The story of earning money through unethical means
All these stories can hinder your ability to attract wealth and live joyfully with money.
